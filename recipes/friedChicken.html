Instructions

  1. Season chicken in a bowl.
  2. Marinate in buttermilk for 6 hours or overnight.
  3. Dredge in seasoned flour.
  4. Fry at 350°F until golden and internal temp is 165°F.
  5. Let rest on a rack before serving.

Tips & FAQs

Why use buttermilk?

It tenderizes and adds tangy flavor while helping create a crispy coating.

Can I use milk?

No — milk doesn't have the acidity to tenderize. You can make faux buttermilk with lemon or vinegar.

Serving Suggestions

  • Yellow Squash Casserole
  • Mom's Baked Mac and Cheese
  • Succotash

Storage

Cool completely, store in airtight containers, refrigerate for 4 days. Reheat in oven for best crispiness.
